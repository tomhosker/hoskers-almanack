\chapter{A Supplement to Johnson's \emph{Dictionary}}

Note that the definitions given below are only necessarily correct in the contexts in which they appear in the poems and songs.

\bigskip

\begin{description}

\item[`birdies']{\emph{birds}}
\item[`birks']{either \emph{birches} or \emph{woods} (comprised mainly of birches)}
