It was evening all afternoon.\footnotetext{Wallace Stevens (1879 -- 1955). \cite{norton}. This is the thireenth of Stevens's famous `Thirteen Ways of Looking at a Blackbird'.}\\*
It was snowing\\
And it was going to snow.\\
The blackbird sat\\*
In the cedar-limbs.
