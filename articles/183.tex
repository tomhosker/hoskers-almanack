The sun himself cannot forget his fellow traveller.\footnotetext{Anon. \cite{odq}. The `fellow traveller' is Sir Francis Drake, who was one of the first men to circumnavigate the globe, thus, in a certain sense, under the geocentric view of things, made the same journey which the sun makes every day.}
