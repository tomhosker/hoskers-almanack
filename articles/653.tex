\settowidth{\versewidth}{And the wheel's kick \& the wind's song \& the white sail's shaking,}
\begin{verse}[\versewidth]
\poemlines{5}
I have seen flowers come in stony places\footnotetext{`An Epilogue', John Masefield, Poet Laureate (1878 -- 1967). \cite{oxfordlarkin}.}\\*
And kind things done by men with ugly faces,\\
And the gold cup won by the worst horse at the races,\\*
So I trust, too.
\end{verse}
